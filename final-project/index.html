Ways to have fun

Most of us are pretty good at having fun. We have plenty of ideas that we are already used to taking advantage of. Most of us know about Netflix, Hulu and other streaming sites. Other people like to play video games. Some people even play board games still. I am here to give you just a few more ideas on how to spend your time having fun during the quarantine. If you want a few new ideas go ahead and click on the “Fun” tab!

Ways to stay productive

The less appealing way to spend our time during this quarantine is by being productive. To be fair we should probably all be more productive all the time anyway, but we all know we have no desire to actually do something that isn’t immediately fun. If you are actually able to pull yourself away from the TV and think about doing some chores here are a few ideas of where to start. Click on the “Productive” tab to get some ideas. This is the goal!
