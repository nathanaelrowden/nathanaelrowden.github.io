Overview

Purpose

I am a very organized person but sometimes I can get lost in the little things. I would like to create a site that helps to organize all sorts of information. Wether you are looking for a way to create an easy schedule or for a place to keep track of a list of your movies or books, this website is for you.

Audience

This will be targeted towards coolege students, but high school students and buisness profesionals could make great use of this tool as well. Individuals could use this for personal plans and to dos. Buisnesses could use this to help organize and plan for meetings and tasks. This could also be used by anyone looking to organize content in to lists such as a list of pros and cons.
