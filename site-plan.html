Typography

Heading Font: Merienda

I think that all headings should stand out. I have always used fonts like Merienda as my fonts in Microsoft Word and I think it really stands out.

Paragraph Font: Lato

I have never used Lato before, but I saw it as an option on this document so I googled it. I really like it and think it is easy on the eyes and simple to read and follow.
